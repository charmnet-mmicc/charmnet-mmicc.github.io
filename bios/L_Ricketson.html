Lee Ricketson is a Staff Scientist in the Center for Applied Scientific Computing at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory. His research develops novel algorithms for simulating kinetic plasma behavior, focusing in particular on overcoming the curse of dimensionality and multiple time-scales present in such systems. He earned his Ph.D. in mathematics at UCLA in 2014, where he was awarded the outstanding dissertation prize from the Pacific Journal of Mathematics.
