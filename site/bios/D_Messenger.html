Daniel Messenger is a Research Associate in the Applied Mathematics Department at the University of Colorado Boulder. He will begin a Director's Postdoctoral Fellowship in the T-5 Group in the Theoretical Division at Los Alamos National Laboratory in Fall 2024. Daniel received his MSc in Applied Mathematics from Simon Fraser University in 2019, and his PhD in Applied Mathematics from University of Colorado Boulder in 2022. His research concerns data-driven modeling with an emphasis on weak-form methods, and interacting particle systems, with applications including plasma physics, cell migration, and ecology.
